Privacy Policy

At Sprunki Game, accessible from https://sprunkr.online/, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information collected by Sprunki and how we utilize it.

If you have any questions or require further information regarding our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Log Files

Sprunki follows standard procedures for using log files. These files log visitors when they access our website. All hosting companies conduct this process as part of their analytics services. The data collected from log files may include Internet Protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, Internet Service Providers (ISPs), date and time stamps, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks. This information is not linked to any personally identifiable information. It is utilized for trend analysis, site administration, user movement tracking on the website, and gathering demographic data.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like many other websites, Sprunki uses 'cookies'. These cookies are implemented to store information such as visitors' preferences and the pages they accessed or visited on our site. This information enhances the user experience by customizing our web page content based on visitors' browser types and other relevant details.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, a third-party vendor on our site, uses cookies known as DART cookies to serve ads to our visitors based on their previous visits to Sprunki and other websites on the internet. Visitors can opt out of the use of DART cookies by visiting the Google ad and content network Privacy Policy at the following URL: Google Privacy Policy.

Privacy Policies of Advertisers

You can consult this list to find the Privacy Policy for each of the advertising partners of Sprunki.

Third-party ad servers or ad networks may utilize technologies such as cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons in their advertisements and links that appear on Sprunki. These technologies are sent directly to users' browsers, and they automatically receive your IP address when this happens. These technologies are employed to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the content you see on various websites.

Please note that Sprunki has no control over, and is not responsible for, the cookies that third-party advertisers may use.

Third Party Privacy Policies

Sprunki's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Therefore, we advise you to review the individual Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information, which may include their practices and instructions on how to opt out of certain options.

You may choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. More detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers can be found on the respective websites of these browsers.

Children's Information

Protecting the privacy of children is another priority for us. We encourage parents and guardians to monitor and guide their children's online activities.

Sprunki does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided such information on our website, please contact us immediately, and we will make every effort to promptly remove such information from our records.

Online Privacy Policy Only

This Privacy Policy applies solely to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website regarding the information they share and/or collect at Sprunki. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or through channels other than this website.

Consent
